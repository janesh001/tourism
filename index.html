About Indian Tourism

India is a land of diverse cultures, languages, traditions, and landscapes. From the snow-capped Himalayas in the north to the tropical beaches in the south, from ancient temples and monuments to modern cities, India offers something for every traveler.

With 40 UNESCO World Heritage Sites, countless festivals, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality, India welcomes millions of tourists every year to experience its incredible heritage and natural beauty.
